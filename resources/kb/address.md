When you signed up you got a chat address that looks like an email: `you@{domain}`. It's your
identity on {brand} — people use it to reach you, and you use it (with the password you chose at
sign-up) to log in to any chat app.

You'll always find it on your [dashboard](/dashboard). It's **permanent** and can't be changed, so
it's worth liking the name you picked.
