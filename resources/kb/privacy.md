title: Privacy & public rooms
category: Good to know
order: 1
summary: Public rooms are public; for sensitive chats, use end-to-end encryption.
keywords: [privacy, public, encryption, omemo, security]
---
- **Public rooms are public.** Anyone in a public room can see what you post there — treat it like a
  room full of people, because it is.
- **For anything sensitive**, use an app with end-to-end (OMEMO) encryption turned on, so only you
  and the other person can read it.
- **Photos and files you share** are stored on our server so others can download them, and are
  automatically deleted about 30 days later (with end-to-end encryption, what's stored is unreadable
  ciphertext).
- How we handle your data is in our [Privacy Policy](/privacy).
