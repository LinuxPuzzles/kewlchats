There are two ways to chat on {brand}, and they share the same account:

- **In your browser** — open [web chat](/chat) and you're in. Nothing to install.
- **On your phone** — install a chat app (**Conversations** on Android, **Monal** on iPhone) and
  sign in with your address and password. Step-by-step setup is on your [dashboard](/dashboard).

Use either, or both — your messages follow you everywhere.
