Here's the trick that surprises everyone: **rooms are addresses too.** A public room looks like
`roomname@{muc}`.

To join one you don't "search" — you add its address, the same way you'd add a person. Some apps
even list it under *Add contact*; others call it *Join group chat* or *Join channel*.

1. Find **Join group chat / channel** (or **Add contact**) in your app.
2. Enter the room's address, e.g. `{lounge}`.
3. Pick a nickname to show in the room, and join.

New here? The Lounge (`{lounge}`) is a good first stop — someone's usually around.
