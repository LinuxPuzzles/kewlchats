People are just addresses. To add someone, you need their full address — for example
`friend@{domain}`.

It even works across our sites: a `@ready2.im` friend and a `@kewlchats.net` friend can add each
other — it's all one community.

1. In your app, look for **Add contact** (or a **+** button).
2. Type their full address and confirm.
3. They'll get a request to accept — once they do, you'll see each other online and can start chatting.

In web chat it's the same idea: hit **+ / Add Contact** and enter their address.
